Armor of Agathys
1st-level abjuration
Casting Time: 1action
Range: Self
Components: V, S, M(a cup of water)
Duration: 1hour
A protective magical force surrounds you, manifesting as a spectral frost that covers you and your gear.
You gain 5temporary hit points for the duration. If a creature hits you with a melee attack while you have these hit points, the creature takes 5cold damage.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, both the temporary hit points and the cold damage increase by 5for each slot level above 1st.
