Regenerate
7th-level transmutation
Casting Time: 1minute
Range: Touch
Components: V, S, M(a prayer wheel and holy water)
Duration: 1hour
You touch a creature and stimulate its natural healing ability. The target regains 4d8 +15 hit points. For the duration of the spell, the target regains 1hit point at the start of each of its turns (10 hit points each minute).
The target's severed body members (fingers, legs, tails, and so on), if any, are restored after 2minutes.
If you have the severed part and hold it to the stump, the spell instantaneously causes the limb to knit to the stump.
