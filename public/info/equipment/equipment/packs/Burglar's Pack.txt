Burglar's Pack
Cost: 16 gp
Description: Includes a backpack, a bag of 1,000 ball bearings, 10 feet of string, a bell, 5candles, a crowbar, a hammer, 10 pitons, a hooded lantern, 2flasks of oil, 5days rations, a tinderbox, and a waterskin. The pack also has 50 feet of hempen rope strapped to the side of it.
