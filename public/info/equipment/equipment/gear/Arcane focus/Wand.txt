Item: Wand 
Cost: 10 gp 
Weight: 1 lb. 
Description: An arcane focus is a special item' an orb, a crystal, a rod, a specially constructed staff, a wand-like length of wood, or some similar item' designed to channel the power of arcane spells. A sorcerer, warlock, or wizard can use such an item as a spellcasting focus, as described in chapter 10.
